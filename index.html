Welcome to P.P. World!

P.P. World is the abbreviation of Post Pandemic World and refers to a virtual world created by TBG, a group of three artists: Eunkyung Kang, Juhee Shin and Yoonsun. TBG imagines how our lives will change after the pandemic, creating P.P. World, a familiar, but new world. TBG aims to aesthetically express an overflowing imagination, to provide pleasure through 2D and 3D graphics technologies, and to offer a metaverse world of the near future to come.

TBG imagines how our lives will change after the pandemic, creating P.P. World, a familiar, but new world. TBG aims to aesthetically express an overflowing imagination, to provide pleasure through 2D and 3D graphics technologies, and to offer a metaverse world of the near future to come.

In this world, we have three regions: Forest, Capital and Desert. Each region represents not only the aspects of our society in the future but also the values we have to keep.

Forest is the nature which has immense possibility that cannot be destroyed. Capital shows our prospects for the near future, mainly with developed biotechnology and neoliberalism. Desert has all the trash we dump that it symbolizes the darkest side of the world, but one of our character, Saza, is born that it gives a message that the hope exists.
